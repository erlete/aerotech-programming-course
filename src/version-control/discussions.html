Introduction

Discussions are forums for your projects. They are useful to discuss ideas, implementations or problems with other developers. They are also useful to reference issues and share information about the project without adding clutter to the issues' interface.

Discussion types

Discussions support different types of content:
  • Announcements: informative posts that can be pinned to the top of the discussion board
  • Q&A: questions that can be answered by other developers
  • Polls: voting systems that can be used to make decisions
  • Custom templates: custom content types that can be created by the maintainers of the repository as deemed necessary
